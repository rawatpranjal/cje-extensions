\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

CVaR-CJE reframes LLM judge evaluation around the failures that matter in deployment. The method keeps the CJE recipe--calibrate, evaluate, audit--but replaces mean outcomes with a lower-tail stop-loss objective. The audit is not optional: a rejected policy is a refusal to make a calibrated level claim. The next step is empirical, not rhetorical: build Arena-Tail, pre-register the alphas and gate, and show that the method separates policies whose means are similar but whose failures are not.

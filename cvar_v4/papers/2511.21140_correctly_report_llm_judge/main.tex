\section*{Impact Statement}

This paper advances the field of machine learning by improving the statistical reliability of LLM-as-a-judge evaluations. By correcting bias from imperfect LLM evaluators and providing uncertainty quantification, our work helps reduce the risk of misleading performance claims and supports more rigorous evaluation practices. The contribution is methodological and does not introduce new models that affect users but instead strengthens existing evaluation pipelines. We view the broader societal impact as positive.

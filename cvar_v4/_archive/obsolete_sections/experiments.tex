\subsection{Result 4: Estimator Comparison}

The estimator comparison should answer a narrow question: does calibrated shortfall estimation improve over raw judge tails and oracle-only tails at the same oracle budget? Report bias, RMSE, coverage, CI length, and audit rejection rate. Do not mix rejected and accepted rows when reporting level-error summaries; use an accepted-only analysis and a separate rejection-power analysis.
